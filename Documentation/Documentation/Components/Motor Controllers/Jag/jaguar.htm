Jaguar Information Site

Designed specifically for the FIRST® Robotics Competition, the Jaguar module allows you to design increasingly complex robots within the short six-week build period. Jaguar will introduce you to the new, lower cost technology standard for advanced motor control. The Jaguar module brings new motor control technology to the competition including integrated limit switch inputs that function in combination with the brake/coast inputs. The Jaguar module also brings several safety features such as monitoring for instantaneous winding current, battery voltage, and ambient temperature, all at the same frequency at which the H-bridge is driven.

As an FRC team member, you will find that the Jaguar module’s enclosure is easy to use and well-designed for competition. The power and motor terminals are color-coded to reduce wiring errors, the enclosure better shields the cooling fan and electronics from debris, and the wire retention tabs keep wires in place.
